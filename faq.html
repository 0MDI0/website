What is MDI Web Services Support (AWS Support)?
AWS Support provides customers with assistance with technical issues and additional guidance for using infrastructures in the AWS Cloud. Customers can choose the level of support that best suits their needs, in keeping with AWS's tradition of delivering the individual components for success without requiring customers to purchase bundled solutions and make long-term commitments. AWS Support is personalized, responsive support provided by experienced technical support professionals. The service helps customers use AWS products and features. Monthly billing and the ability to create an unlimited number of support tickets free customers from long-term commitments. Customers can contact support with operational or technical questions and receive personalized assistance with predictable response times.
How is AWS Support Advanced Tiers different from Basic Support?
AWS Basic Support provides all AWS customers with access to the Resource Center, Service Status Dashboard, Product FAQ, Forums, and Health Check Support at no additional cost. Customers who wish to receive extended support can subscribe to AWS Support Developer, Business, Enterprise Transitional, or Enterprise. Customers who choose AWS Support receive personalized, responsive support from AWS engineers. The service helps customers use AWS products and features. Monthly billing and the ability to create an unlimited number of support tickets free customers from long-term commitments. Customers can contact support with operational or technical questions and receive personalized assistance with predictable response times.
What level of architectural support does AWS Support provide?
The level of architecture support provided depends on the overall level of support. The higher the service level, the broader the support for specific use cases based on the characteristics of customer applications. Developer Plan: Components A guide to sharing all AWS products, features, and services. Guidance, recommendations and general advice on architecture. Business Plan: Use Case Considerations Guidelines for choosing AWS products, features, and services for your specific use case. Recommendations for optimizing AWS products and configuring them for your specific requirements. Enterprise Transition Plan: Application Architecture Consulting partnership to support specific applications and use cases. Includes design evaluation and architecture recommendations. Corporate Transition Plan: The customer support team includes a pool of personal technical managers. Enterprise Plan: Application Architecture Consulting partnership to support specific applications and use cases. Includes design evaluation and architecture recommendations. Enterprise-level customer support includes a dedicated technical manager and access to an AWS solution architect.
I only use one or two services. Can I purchase support only for the ones I use?
No. Our support program covers the full range of AWS services. When designing AWS Support, we took into account the fact that many of our customers use multiple infrastructure web services at the same time within a single application. We have found that most of the support issues that users of multiple services have are related to sharing those services. Our goal is to provide customers with the best application support possible.
How many support cases can I open in AWS Support?
As much as you want. Basic support plan customers can only create customer support tickets and service limit increases.
How many users can create support tickets?
The Business, Enterprise Transitional, and Enterprise support plans have no limits on the number of users that can create technical support tickets (powered by AWS Identity and Access Management - IAM). Only one user can create technical support tickets under the Developer Support Plan. You cannot create technical support tickets under the Basic support plan.
